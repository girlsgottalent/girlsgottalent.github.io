What? OMG! The title is daunting. But after you read this article on Fortune, you will realize - OK, this isn't as violent as it sounds. Instead, it's fascinating!

Elizabeth Holmes is stunningly beautiful (unfairly so! See her picture below) and amazingly brilliant. When she was a college sophomore, she invented a wearable skin patch that could give people medicine and radio your doctor what's going on in your blood in real time - then adjust the amount of medicine accordingly. Now, ten years later, she's the owner of Theranos, a company in the $73 billion diagnostic lab business, which may radically change our health care system. The company can do more blood tests using 100 times less blood than current tests need - at a 10th of the price!

"I don't want to make an incremental change in some technology in my life, I want to create a whole new technology, and one that is aimed at helping humanity at all levels."

This girl is the perfect image of Beauty and Brains, and exceptionally brave! If you haven't already heard about Theranos, wait another year or two and see what happens.
