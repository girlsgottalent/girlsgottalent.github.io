Photographer and marine ecologist Clare Fieseler's series "Outnumbered" features women scientists who do things like competitive cycling and work knee-deep in muddy swamps - they're tough, but they're women just the same. Fieseler wants to build confidence for women in science. It's entirely possible for girls to do science and have fun; just look at all these women who are already doing it!
