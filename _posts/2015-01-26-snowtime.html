This is an amazing time-lapse film of the formation of snowflakes, created by filmmaker Vyascheslav Ivanov. How does this happen? Tiny water droplets high up in the atmosphere meet other tiny particles in the air and start to form crystals. As more tiny water droplets freeze onto the crystal, the snowflake grows!

Those of you on the East Coast - stay warm and safe during the blizzard!
