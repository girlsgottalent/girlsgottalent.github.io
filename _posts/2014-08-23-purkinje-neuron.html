Source: Biomedical Picture of the Day

The green thing that looks like a music note is a Purkinje neuron, a nerve cell in the brain. This particular cell acts as a messenger, sending tiny chemical packages between immune cells and other types of neurons when we're sick. Here the cell was told to make green fluorescent protein - giving it that beautiful green glow.
