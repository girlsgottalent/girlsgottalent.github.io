It's a rainy day where I come from today. I happened to see this on Tumblr: what makes up the smell of rain? Turns out it's a bunch of things, including chemicals called ozone, petrichor, and geosmin - which can come from rocks, lightning breaking oxygen molecules in the air, or bacteria.
