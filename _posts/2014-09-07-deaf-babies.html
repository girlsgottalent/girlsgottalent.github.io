So precious! Cooper has been born deaf and hears his mom talking for the first time ever in his life. This was made a reality because Cooper got cochlear implants, which bypasses the damaged ear and transmits sounds electronically to the auditory nerve itself. The brain can still process sounds, and it feels like you're hearing!
