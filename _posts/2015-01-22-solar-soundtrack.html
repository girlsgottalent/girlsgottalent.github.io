A solar flare from August 31, 2012. This can be transformed into sound.

Have you wondered whether it's possible to "hear" movement? Turns out this has actually happened! Robert Alexander translated dancers' movements into a soundtrack. NASA found out and hired him to turn the movements of the Sun into soundtracks also. Scientists can analyze the sound files for unusual patterns or clues that help them find out what is happening on the Sun. Science can be an art!
